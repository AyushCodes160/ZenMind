Questions

Everything you might wonder about

Is Zen AI a replacement for real therapy?
No — Zen AI is a compassionate wellness companion, not a clinical therapist. It's designed for daily emotional support, coping strategies, and mindfulness. For serious mental health concerns, we always recommend working with a licensed professional.
Is my data and conversation private?
Absolutely. Your conversations with Zen AI and your journal entries are completely private. We use end-to-end encryption and never share your data with third parties. Your wellbeing is ours to protect.
I've never meditated before — is this for me?
Yes, completely! Zen Alpha is built with beginners in mind. Every session starts simple — even just 3 minutes of breathing. There's no right or wrong way to begin; we meet you exactly where you are.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, cancel any time with no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period. Your journal entries and progress are yours to export forever.
How is Zen AI different from other chatbots?
Zen is built specifically for emotional wellness. It always validates feelings before offering suggestions, uses evidence-based conversational techniques, and understands the context of your journey over time — it's a companion, not just a Q&A bot.
Is the community safe and moderated?
Yes. Our community is actively moderated by trained wellness advocates. We have clear guidelines against judgment or negativity, and every post is reviewed. This is a space built entirely on kindness.
